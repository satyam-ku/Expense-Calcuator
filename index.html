How It Works

Normal

Personal debt tracker.

Key Feature: Money you record as "I GAVE" here is automatically deducted from your Personal Budget in the Expenses tab. Use this for lending money to friends.

General

Isolated record keeping.

Key Feature: Transactions here do NOT affect your Personal Budget. Use this for business records or tracking money that doesn't come out of your daily pocket.

Interest

Investment tracker.

Key Feature: Automatically calculates repayment amount based on:

  • Principal Amount
  • Rate %
  • Duration & Frequency
  • Type (Simple vs Compound)

Group

Split-wise logic.

Key Feature: You select members from your existing "Normal" list. Any transaction added to the Group is divided equally and added to each member's individual ledger.
